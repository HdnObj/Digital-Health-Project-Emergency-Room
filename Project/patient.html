Understanding Your Vitals
Blood Pressure
Measures the force of blood against artery walls. Normal range: 90/60 – 120/80 mmHg
Heart Rate
Number of heartbeats per minute. Normal range: 60 – 100 bpm
Oxygen Saturation (SpO₂)
Percentage of oxygen in your blood. Normal range: 95 – 100%
Body Temperature
Core body heat measurement. Normal range: 36.1 – 37.2°C
